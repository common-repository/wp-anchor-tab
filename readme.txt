A simple plugin to integrate your Anchor Tab with your WordPress blog.

== Description ==

This is a simple plugin to get your [Anchor Tab](https://anchortab.com) working on your WordPress blog.
After creating an Anchor Tab using the [Tab Manager](https://anchortab.com/manager), all you need to do is
install this plugin, then go to your "Anchor Tab" settings in WordPress. Tell the plugin your Tab ID, and
you're off to the races.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder containing wp-anchortab.php and settings.php to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to "Anchor Tab" on the left side of the WordPress admin section, and enter your Tab ID.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is an Anchor Tab? How do I get one? =

Glad you asked. Anchor Tab is an email opt-in tool that integrates with email marketing services. People
enter their email on your Anchor Tab, it appears on your email service. No fuss. To get one, just go to
[anchortab.com](https://anchortab.com) and create an account.
